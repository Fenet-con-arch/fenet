Introduction

Welcome. This website is designed with emerging technology's research concerning about Digital Transformation (DT) in particular, which is a journey with multiple connected intermediary goals. Its strategy aims to create the capabilities of fully leveraging the possibilities and opportunities of new technologies and their impact faster, better and in more innovative way in the future. The assignment explore the essence of digital transformation as a vision to take this journey, its evolution and how it is present across various business processes and industries. In general topics included are definition of DT, matter of DT in different situation, DT framework, drivers of DT and key trends in DT.
